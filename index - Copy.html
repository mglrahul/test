Web of Sites is a group of websites created by Katharine Wall in Vancouver, Canada.

The Web of Sites portfolio began in 2005 when I first created Adopted.com to help Adoptees locate their birth parents. My group of sites has since grown to include a dating site for people with Lyme disease, an affiliate site aggregating Wrap Dresses from all over the world, and an exciting and top secret new app still under development!

Adopted.com

For over 10 years now Adopted.com has been a leading Adoption Reunion website, bringing together adoptees and birth parents all over the world. With a special algorithm designed to help people in all kinds of adoption situations, Adopted.com offers an incredible tool for people to locate their family that can't be found anywhere else.

LymeLife.com

With the recent discovery that Lyme Disease is spread as an STD, there rose an immediate need for a site for people with Lyme to network for information and companionship. I responded to this need by creating LymeLife.com, which has the destinction of being the only dating site in the world dedicated to those with Lyme.
